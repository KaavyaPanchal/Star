About Us

About Us

Welcome to StarOverSeas. In phase 1, SOL initiated production of Home usage Aluminum Foil. As of today majority of these products are imported and SOL shall provide the solution for import substitution as well as create an export opportunity for these products as they are in high demand in neighbouring countries. In phase 2, the company has identified products which are imported in high quantities which include Kitchen Towels, Serviettes and Toilet roll. The Paper core planned for production will reduce our import dependency and reduction of cost for the paper core which is very essential for the packaging of the products planned. Similarly the Paper cups will be made with PLA coated paper which is eco friendly and in line with REMA (Rwanda Environmental Management Authority) directives on single use plastics.

Our Mission

Deal in products which can be recycled thereby helping the broader objectives of sustainability along with creating new opportunities, Import Substitution and Exports opportunity and develop skilled manpower.
